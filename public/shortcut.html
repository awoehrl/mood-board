Share to Mood Board

Add links, text, and images from any app on your iPhone.

Install iOS Shortcut Back to Board

What it captures

Links Selected text Articles Images

When you share text from Safari, the page URL is automatically included. You pick which room to add it to.

After installing

  1. Open any app (Safari, Instagram, Pinterest...)
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Mood Board
  4. Pick a room — done!

Tip: Move it up in your share sheet

In the share sheet, scroll to the end of the actions list and tap Edit Actions. Move "Add to Mood Board" to your favorites so it appears at the top.
