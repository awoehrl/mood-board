+ Mood Board Bookmarklet

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⬆ Drag me to bookmarks bar

How to install:

  1. Make sure your Bookmarks Bar is visible (View → Show Bookmarks Bar)
  2. Drag the blue button above into your bookmarks bar
  3. That's it! Click it on any page to add it to your mood board

What it does:

  1. Shows a floating zone picker on the current page
  2. You pick a room
  3. The page URL + title get added to that zone
